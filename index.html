Welcome!

Joshua is a senior data scientist at C3 AI. His research interests include computational social choice, multi-agent systems, and AI ethics. Previously, Joshua worked at the RAND Corporation. Joshua earned his Ph.D. in computer science from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, under the advisement of Lirong Xia, and B.S. in data science engineering from the University of Michigan.
